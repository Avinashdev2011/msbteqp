Privacy Policy

At MSBTE QP, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website to access MSBTE previous question papers and related resources.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our website:

  • Non-personal information such as device type, browser type, and IP address to improve user experience and website functionality.
  • Information you provide when interacting with features such as filters or departmental pages.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for:

  • Providing you with the most relevant MSBTE exam papers based on the department or course you are interested in.
  • Improving the overall experience on the website.
  • Tracking website usage to enhance our content and services.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance the user experience on our website. Cookies help us remember your preferences and improve the usability of the website. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Third-Party Services

Our website may display advertisements served by third parties, such as Google AdSense. These third parties may collect information about your activities on our site for serving relevant ads. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to access, modify, or request the deletion of your personal data. For any queries or requests regarding your personal data, please contact us at contactus@msbteqp.avibey.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
